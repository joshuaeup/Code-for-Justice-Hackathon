Our beginnings

Environmental Justice Association was founded in 2020 by a group of students. Their inspiration for starting the organization can be attributed to our local and global climate changing drastically. We proudly work with business, government agencies, and other nonprofit organizations to reduce the amount of emissions and waste we produce.

Our Mission

Environmental Justice Association is a youth led, nonprofit organization with a strong emphasis on green living and reduction of carbon waste.
