Where do my donations go?

The goal of the Enviromental Justice Association is to try to make the earth a better place, it's that simple.
All of our proceeds and donations go straight into our enviromental involvement. What that means
is that all our efforts go into things like planting trees, cleaning up trash,
and fighting for our earth! This is our planet our home, we are all in this together
